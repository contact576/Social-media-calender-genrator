## 6. The scripts

Three flagship reels, written shot-by-shot. Each has all three hooks (what they *see*, *hear*, and *read* in the first second), and each is built on a decoded winner + your real proof.

### Script 1 — "Same $1,000" · REACH · 35s

- **Spoken hook (0–2s):** "Same thousand-dollar budget. One agency got zero booked jobs — we got thirty-seven leads. Here's the difference."
- **Visual hook:** hard split screen. LEFT (red): a boosted post, label "BOOSTED — 0 booked jobs". RIGHT (green): a Google Ads dashboard, label "STRUCTURED — 37 leads". "$1,000/mo" stamped on *both* sides.
- **Overlay hook (≤8 words):** "Same $1,000. 37 leads vs 0."
- **Body:** (1) hold the split — "more leads isn't more budget." (2) wipe to the real result — "we didn't touch the spend, we fixed the structure: campaign build, high-intent keywords, one conversion landing page." (3) stamps: "Mo1: 37 leads" → "Mo2: 3 deals = $150K."
- **CTA:** "If your ads bring clicks but no jobs, comment STRUCTURE and I'll send the 3-part build."
- **Why it works:** transplants the 20.5× scoreboard format onto your real contractor case. **A competitor can't post this — they don't have the result.**

### Script 2 — "POV: your ads don't convert" · NURTURE · 8s

- **Spoken hook:** none — trending audio (this format wins silent).
- **Visual hook:** over-the-shoulder shot at a real desk, two monitors with a Google Ads dashboard, coffee + sticky notes, dim "2am operator" light. Fast ~1s cuts.
- **Overlay hook:** "POV: your ads don't convert. Fix this."
- **Body (rapid text cards):** "1. Landing page asks for everything → ask for ONE thing." · "2. Offer is 'contact us' → give a reason to act now." · "3. You reply in 12 hours → speed-to-lead is under 5 min." · "Structure beats budget."
- **CTA:** "Save this. Comment AUDIT for a free funnel teardown."
- **Why it works:** the 27.6× ultra-short save-bait format; built to loop (last card cuts to the first).

### Script 3 — "42 patients from Google" · CONVERT · 40s (Hinglish cut available)

- **Spoken hook (0–2s):** "This physio clinic was getting walk-ins and hope. Last month: forty-two patients booked from Google. Here's the exact setup."
